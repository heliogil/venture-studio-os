# Zebra Shield

**Bankroll intelligence for sports bettors who want to stop tilting and start tracking.**

## Identity

- **Tagline:** "Stop tilting. Start tracking."
- **Market:** English-first (EN: X, Reddit, YouTube Shorts); Portuguese secondary
- **Domain:** gozebra.bet
- **Archetype:** The Intelligent Guardian of the Underdog — discipline + dark humour, not fintech seriousness

## What it does

Zebra Shield is NOT a prediction engine. It's a bankroll management platform:

- Tracks betting history imported from exchange CSV or manual paste
- Calculates Discipline Score, ROI with vs without discipline, drawdown, stake distribution
- Recommends Kelly fractional stake sizing (25% fractional default)
- Sets configurable stop-loss alerts (daily -5% default)
- Displays whether the user's losses were bad luck or bad process

**The positioning:** "The model finds the edge. The system makes sure you don't go broke before you realise it."
